1. Introduction

Welcome to ToyBox - Starter Pack ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive when you use our ToyBox - Starter Pack mobile application (the "App").

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the App, including:

  • Images and Photos: The photos you upload to transform into toy figures using our App.
  • User Generated Content: The toy figures, customizations, and other content you create using our App.
  • In-App Preferences: Your preferences and settings within the App.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage of the App, including:

  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device such as device model, operating system, version, unique device identifiers (such as IDFV - Identifier for Vendors), and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, such as feature usage, time spent in the App, and user interactions.
  • Error Reports: Information about crashes or system activity when the App isn't working correctly.
  • Analytics Data: App performance metrics and usage statistics to improve our services.

2.3 Subscription and Purchase Information

If you subscribe to our premium features or make in-app purchases, we collect transaction information including:

  • Subscription type and duration
  • Purchase date and renewal information
  • Transaction identifiers

Payment information is processed by our third-party payment processor (RevenueCat). We do not store your complete payment information but may receive certain transaction details to manage your subscription.

2.4 Facial Data

Our app may collect and process facial data when you upload photos to create personalized toy figures. This facial data:

  • What we collect: Facial features and characteristics from the photos you upload
  • Purpose: Is used solely to generate personalized toy figures based on your uploaded photos
  • Storage: Is stored securely on our servers and with our service provider Segmind
  • Retention: Is retained only for the duration necessary to process your request and for a limited time to improve your experience (typically less than 30 days)
  • Sharing: Is never sold to third parties and only shared with our service providers as described in section 4.1
  • Deletion: Can be deleted upon request as described in section 7.2

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your facial data.

Important: We do not collect or store sensitive personal information like your social security number, government ID, password, or financial account numbers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App functionality
  • Process and fulfill your requests to transform photos into toy figures
  • Process your transactions and manage your subscription
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our App
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Debug to identify and repair errors in our App
  • Protect the rights, privacy, safety, and property of ToyBox - Starter Pack, you, and others

Note: Your photos and images are NOT used to train AI models. They are only used to provide you with the service you request.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share the information we collect in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors, service providers, and contractors who perform services for us or on our behalf, including:

  • Apple Inc.: For app distribution, payment processing, and analytics. Apple may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: For data storage and analytics
  • Segmind: For AI processing of your images. Segmind processes your facial data in accordance with its privacy policy (available at http://www.segmind.com). Their systems store your data securely and typically retain it for no longer than three (3) months past the termination of your use of our service.
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

4.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

Important: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We store your information for as long as necessary to provide you with the App and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. This includes:

  • Images you upload are stored for processing purposes and may be retained in our systems for a limited time (typically up to 30 days) to improve your experience.
  • Facial data is retained only for the duration necessary to process your request and is deleted within 30 days after processing.
  • User-generated toy figures and content are retained as long as you maintain your usage of the App.
  • Usage data may be retained for analytics purposes to improve our services.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at muratcancicekk@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: We limit access to personal information to employees and service providers who need access to perform their duties
  • Regular Security Reviews: We regularly review and update our security practices
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored on secure servers with appropriate safeguards

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Update

As our App does not require account creation, most data associated with your usage is stored locally on your device. For any server-side data, you can contact us to request access or updates.

7.2 Deletion

You can request deletion of your uploaded images, facial data, and created content by contacting us at muratcancicekk@gmail.com.

7.3 Subscription Management

You can manage your subscription, including cancellation and refund requests, through your device's app store account settings.

7.4 Device Permissions

You can manage the App's access to your device features (like camera and photo library) through your device settings.

7.5 European Union Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following additional rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your data to another service
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your personal data

7.6 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request information about what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

Note: We do not sell personal information to third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ in the App Store, which means it may be used by users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child under 13 without your consent, please contact us at muratcancicekk@gmail.com, and we will take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.

We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy and, where required by law, we may provide you with more prominent notice or obtain your consent.

Your continued use of the App after any changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
